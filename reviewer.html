Drug Safety Reviewer Tool

Enterprise tool that leverages ML models to reduce the time it takes case analysts to review literature review articles by 33% and reduce duplicate cases.

Product Design User Research Interaction Design

Context

When a pharmaceutical company releases a drug product, such as the COVID vaccine, they are required to monitor adverse effects and report them to regulatory bodies such as the FDA. This process is called pharmacovigilance, also known as drug safety. Clinical research organizations such as Parexel are often contracted by these companies to do this type of monitoring activity.

For a single product, dozens to hundreds of case processors may review thousands of articles each week to identify if there are any Individual Case Safety Reports (ICSRs) that need to be reported. This tool, Reviewer, helps case processors upload articles and uses ML models to detect drug names, potential patients, and descriptions of side effects, saving hundreds of hours each week.

Reviewer is used by hundreds of users and supports more than a dozen pharmaceutical companies (names redacted for confidentiality).

My Role

Product design, user research, user testing, interaction design

Problems tackled

  • I reordered the Import process to make it easier to understand which files failed.
