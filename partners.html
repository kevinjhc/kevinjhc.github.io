Eczema Tracking App

This prototype app helps parents manage their child’s eczema.

UI/UX Prototyping Healthcare

About this project

At Partners Healthcare, I worked with a dermatologist to design this concept app. This app is meant to help parents manage their child's eczema by allowing them to log flares and visualize trends over time.

Design Challenges
How might we make it easy for parents to log and understand trends in their child's eczema symptoms?

How might we leverage this data to extend the field's understanding of eczema?
