My name is Kevin, and I am a product designer from Philadelphia

Since 2011, I've spent my career validating design decisions, listening to users, and designing web and mobile interfaces and experiences -- all in service of making people's lives better.

I'm lucky to have worked with such talented product managers, engineers, and designers that challenge me to become better at my craft.

I've designed products in a variety of industries and with companies of all sizes, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. One of those products includes a company that created an an app with a 4.4/5 rating and over 4 million downloads.

I'd love to hear more about the problems you are trying to tackle for your users. Please reach out if you'd like to work together!
