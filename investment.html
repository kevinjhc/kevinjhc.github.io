Investment Portfolio App

This prototype app helps novice investors simplify and understand how their investments are performing.

UI/UX Data Vis Fintech

About this project

I designed this app out of a personal need to simplify the way I view my investment portfolio. Previous services I've used, such as Fidelity and Betterment, obscure key information and make it hard for me to understand how each holding moves the larger portfolio. They also make it challenging for me to compare my returns against other benchmarks.

This app clearly shows my rate of return and how each company and sector is moving so I can easily understand high-level historical trends.
