Ubersense

Helping athletes improve using slow motion video

I led the design for Ubersense, an iOS and Android app that lets athletes to record themselves in slow motion and use analysis tools to help them improve.

About Ubersense

Ubersense (now Hudl Technique) is an app that lets athletes record themselves and play video in slow motion so that they can analyze their technique.

Responsibilities
  • User interviews, customer visits, creating prototypes, UI/UX
  • Design iOS and Android features including:
    • My Videos
    • Slow motion wheel UI
    • Analysis tools
    • Tagging videos
    • Sharing videos between athletes and coaches
    • Commenting back and forth on videos between athletes and coaches
    • Posting videos to the "Ubersense Community"
