How can I help you?

I'll partner with you to deliver exceptional digital product design from inception to launch.

1

Design Audits 1 - 2 weeks

Does your team need an outside perspective? Are you struggling to understand why your product isn’t converting users?

  • →
    I'll identify where your product's UX can be improved by providing specific recommendations.
  • →
    The topics I'll address include: usability, user flows, visual design, copywriting, and information architecture.
2

User Research & Usability Testing 2 - 6 weeks

Are you targeting the right users? How do your users actually use your product? Improve your designs by understanding your users' goals and behaviors and seeing how they interact with your products.

  • →
    I’ll create and execute research plans to help you learn more about your existing and prospective users.
  • →
    I'll help you interpret the research insights and help you prioritize which ones to focus on.
3

Interactive Prototypes 1 - 3 weeks

Do you have an idea that you want to validate before committing resources to building it? Do you have multiple designs that you want to compare?

  • →
    I'll create prototypes that you can use to get feedback from users.
  • →
    The level of fidelity depends on your needs -- from low fidelity sketches to high fidelity codes prototypes with mock data.
4

UI / UX Design 2 weeks - 6 months

Looking for a full-service designer?

  • →
    I'll work with you from beginning to end by taking product requirements and turning them into user flows, wireframes and high-fidelity UI.
5

Style Guides & Design Systems 1 - 6 months

Do you need help creating consistency in your designs?

  • →
    I'll help you create reusable systems so that your designers can create a cohesive product together.
  • →
    I'll help you bridge the gap between designers and developers by creating a vanilla HTML/CSS/JS or React-based design system.
6

Development 3 weeks +

Whether it's creating code prototypes or creating an MVP product, I'll help you turn designs into a coded product that users can use.
